Collabrains

Collabrains is a dedicated platform to help people who desire to find training programs organized by various institutions. Collabrains itself is a web-based platform which provides information related to training programs in the field of Computer Science. Collabrains was presented in 2 occasions:
1. Research Topics in Computer Science: is one of the course that I took during my 4th semester. Formed a group consists of 4 and obliged to carry out a research and write a paper about it. The paper title which my friends and I have decided was “Collabrains - Implementation of Web Scraping to Help Find an Available Training Programs”. I was involved in the making of scientific paper for Collabrains by reading 9 different references about Web Scraping as the sources before we write the paper.
2. Student's Local Market Experience (SLME) 2020 through Bisanara.com: SLME is the part of BINUS University’s annual event called BINUS Festival (BiFest). The event is where we are given the opportunity to learn how to market our own product. Formed a group consist of 4 and I was involved on arranging social media contents for promoting Collabrains in several social media platforms. I create all contents using Adobe Photoshop 2020.
